FAQs

SALT90 Gallery Published on August 19, 2019

Are these original artworks?

Yes. These are all original artworks, either digitally created or hand painted. These are copyrighted works and exclusively licensed to SALT90.

Is SALT90 a company?

No. It is a collection or gallery of licensed artworks. It represents multiple art-creators. SALT90's contract with art-creators are non exclusive.

Why do you sell through 3rd party sites?

We sell quality prints and products like printed tee or tote bag through 3rd party vendors like Redbubble. It helps us to deliver quality products worldwide in a cost-effective way.
